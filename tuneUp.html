TUNE UPS

Preventative upkeep and tune-ups can easily extend the life of your vehicle and can save you money in the long run by replacing engine parts that are susceptible to normal wear and tear. Regular tune-ups protect against future engine problems, while identifying and replacing worn materials. Maintaining a normal tune-up schedule according to your vehicle’s owner’s manual will aid in your vehicle lasting longer, sustaining better fuel mileage and enhancing efficiency.

A complete tune-up at Harvey’s includes:

  • Replacement of air filter
  • Replacement of fuel filter
  • Installation of new spark plugs and replacement of worn ignition system wires
  • Evaluation of electronic ignition system
  • Inspection of all fluids and replacement as needed
  • Replacement of cabin air filter
  • Evaluation of battery and cleaning of terminals
  • Evaluation of power crankcase ventilation system and replacement
  • Overall evaluation of vehicle performance
