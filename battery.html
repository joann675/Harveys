Battery and Electrical Services

Whether it’s a dead battery, misfiring starter or alternator, come to Harvey’s for all of your auto electrical repair and maintenance needs. Our services include:

Alternators: An alternator is an automotive device that converts mechanical energy to electric energy (ac). Alternators are important parts of your car's engine and their failure would translate to your car's failure. When your car's alternator develops problems, give Harvey’s a call.

Batteries: The battery supplies electric energy which powers the ignition system, the starter motor and the lights of your vehicle. If you need a jump-start, your battery could be failing. When a battery starts deteriorating, it can either be replaced or repaired - this will depend on the severity of the damage. If you are having issues with your battery, contact Harvey’s. We provide a convenient battery testing and replacement service to get you back on the road. Battery replacement, charging, installation, testing and recycling - we handle it all.

Starters: A starter is a motor (electric, hydraulic or pneumatic) that rotates the internal combustion engine to initiate the engine's operation. Starters are important parts of your vehicle's engine and their failure will translate to your vehicle’s failure. If your starter develops problems, seek expert help by contacting Harvey’s.

At Harvey’s, we can handle all your vehicle’s electrical needs to get you back on the road!
