Harvey’s Auto Service - Your One-Stop Shop for Complete Automotive Maintenance and Repair Services

Harvey’s Auto provides top-quality automotive maintenance and repair services and has served the Lower Cape and surrounding areas for over 40 years. We are dedicated to building loyal relationships with our customers by our commitment to understanding your needs and concerns and by providing quality workmanship. We have established a reputation for honesty and dependable automotive services at a fair price, offering a full set of maintenance and repair services to keep your vehicle safe, reliable and running smoothly Our goal is to take care of your automotive maintenance and repair needs in a timely manner with the best possible quality and service, so whether you’re coming in for your regular oil change or for more complex repair services, you can always count on Harvey’s for honest advice and expert service.


Harvey’s Auto is a licensed Massachusetts Inspection Station and although walk-ins are accepted on a first come first serve basis, appointments are encouraged and can be made by calling (508) 255-5133.
