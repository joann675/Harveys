Computer Diagnosis and Check Engine Light Services

Today’s vehicles are run by computers. Harvey’s maintains the industry leading computer diagnostic equipment to allow our technicians to efficiently and accurately diagnose check engine lights. This equipment allows us to communicate directly with any vehicle’s computer and quickly get to the root cause of the problem. Unlike other shops, this allows us to save you time and money by quickly pinpointing your car’s problem and fixing it the first time.

Let our expert technicians diagnose and repair any part of your vehicle’s electronic system to ensure your vehicle is in top driving condition. Our services include:

Check Engine Light: No one likes to see their vehicle's "Check Engine" light come on.The light could mean a costly problem, like a bad catalytic converter, or it could be something minor, like a loose gas cap. At Harvey’s we can diagnose and repair the problem for you.

Computer: Today, vehicle manufacturers include at least one computer in motor vehicles. A computer's main function is to monitor and adjust engine and transmission operations. The computer receives input information from different sensors. It controls spark plugs, idle speed, and fuel injectors so as to get you the best possible performance. Failure of the computer will greatly affect the things it controls, so to get the most out of your vehicle's computer, have it evaluated regularly for updates and proper operation by Harvey’s.

Diagnostics: Our technicians are able to diagnose all of the electronic parts in your vehicle so for expert diagnostics services, call Harvey’s today.

Drivability: Drivability is the degree of smoothness and steadiness of your vehicle's acceleration. Most modern vehicles are fitted with on-board computers that continuouslycheck the readings from various sensors. In case of drivability problems, you will receive a warning message on your vehicle's dashboard. To have your vehicle diagnosed for drivability issues, give Harvey’s a call. We have the necessary tools and technology to diagnose and address your vehicle's drivability issues.

Engine Controls: Basically, an engine control system is an on-board computer in your vehicle. Like all computers, the engine controls are composed of hardware and software. Automotive engine control systems are responsible for regulating exhaust emissions and providing increased fuel economy. To get the best out of your vehicle's incredibly powerful technology, make sure engine controls are working properly by calling Harvey’s today.
