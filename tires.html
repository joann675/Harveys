Tire Services

Maximizing tire life can be summarized in one word: rotation. Regular rotation improves the ride and handling of your car and most importantly, it provides you maximum tread wear. Rotating your tires according to manufacturer's recommendations promotes even wear and prolongs their life. Make this part of your oil change routine by having your tires rotated every 7,000 to 10,000 miles.

We’ll also inspect your tire tread wear and provide recommendations should your tires need replacement. Old tires can cause your car to hydroplane, slip in snowy conditions or prevent your ability to stop short, all of which affect your ability to control of the vehicle. At Harvey’s we can take care of all of your tire needs providing you with optimum vehicle handling and performance and maximum tread life. We offer a complete range of tire services, including tire sales, mounting, balancing and tire repair. Even if you choose to purchase tires yourself, we offer tire mounting services. Just give us a call!
