Brake Service and Repairs

A fully functioning brake system is crucial for your safety, the safety of your passengers and the safety of other drivers on the road. You should keep your brakes in good working condition by following the manufacturer brake service schedules and by having visual inspections performed routinely. Repair services include:

  • Resurfacing or replacement of brake rotors or drums
  • Master cylinder repair or replacement
  • Brake fluid and caliper replacement
