Air Conditioning Systems

Your vehicleâ€™s air conditioning system plays an important part in providing you and your passengers with both comfort and safety. The auto air conditioning system is responsible for cooling, heating, defrosting, air filtering and humidity control. It provides comfort during the hot summer and ensures that you have a clear view of the road in the damp, cold winter months. Your AC system is a great convenience that is often taken for granted. We have the technical knowledge to fully diagnose, repair and maintain these systems, including refrigerant recharge, compressor replacement, drive belt replacement or leak detection.

Signs that your air conditioning system needs service:

  • Blows hot air when set to cold
  • Runs constantly, but never gets cold
  • Inconsistent temperature control
  • Makes odd noises when operating
  • Vent air volume weaker than normal
  • Stale, moldy or damp smell from vents
